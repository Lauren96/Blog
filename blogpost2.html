Defining Your Style

Having your own unique style is important for artists. It creates a sense of branding for our work that makes people see it and say,'Oh, I know this!' However, this also creates a lot of stress for beginners. Establishing your style will come naturally over time as you learn and refine your skills, so don't be afraid to try well known styles and see which kinds you resonate with! The 'you' in your brand is cultivated over time, and will be a mix of things you've seen with your own twist on it.

Basic Anatomy

Anatomy is a funny thing, especially in cartooning. If the anatomy is off just a little bit, your character will look wrong and wonky. However, if you exaggerate anatomy, the character will look interesting and iconic! There is a decision to make when making a cartoon: Do I make this look somewhat realistic, or completely obnoxious?
